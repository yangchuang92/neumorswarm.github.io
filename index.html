Neumorswarm

Neumorswarm is short for "Neuromorphic Swarm", which is an ongoing research project first proposed by Chuang YANG in March 2020. Neumorswarm aims to fuse neural basis of social animals swarm, neuromorphic computing, and neuromorphic devices to develop a new class of brain-like swarm architecture of Robotics.
